# Moments Calendar 📅

A personal photo calendar Android app built with React & Capacitor.

## Features
- 🖼️ One photo per month
- 🎯 Add events with colors & notes  
- 🔔 Smart reminders — 3 days, 2 days, 1 day & on the day
- 🌙 Light & Dark theme that adapts to your photo
- 📴 100% offline — your data stays on your phone
